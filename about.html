About Us

Atlanta Courtesy Limo’s steadfast commitment to upholding the highest standards of excellence is unparalleled. By setting the precedent for chauffeured services, Atlanta Courtesy Limo has become synonymous with premier chauffeuring.

Atlanta Courtesy Limo has consistently led Atlanta’s transportation industry. Our reputation for reliability, professionalism, and unwavering commitment to chauffeured services has solidified us as the preferred choice for executive transportation needs. In essence, we provide a comprehensive transportation solution with the largest fleet in Atlanta. The Atlanta Courtesy Limo fleet showcases only the finest, latest-model luxury vehicles, including the distinctive Cadillac XTS-L sedans, limousines, SUVs, vans, and mini buses.

Atlanta Courtesy Limo enforces stringent performance standards across reservations, billing, operations, and vehicle maintenance. Additionally, our dedication to the highest level of accountability is demonstrated through rigorous training for all chauffeurs.

Before becoming official “Atlanta Courtesy Limo Chauffeurs,” each chauffeur undergoes extensive background checks, defensive driving certification, and thorough safety training. We cordially invite you to experience the exceptional services of Atlanta Courtesy Limo, offering the convenience of online reservations or through the user-friendly Jilo Ride iPhone or Android app. We eagerly anticipate further opportunities to showcase our commitment to excellence and sincerely thank you for exploring our website.
